Shopping Malls

The reccomended shopping malls are some of the best ones in Liverpool

The shopping malls shown are not sorted in any order, as one is as good as the other.

We pay attention to Google rating, types of shops inside, restaurants and cafeterias, etc.

New Mersey Shopping

New Mersey Shopping is an out of town retail park located in Speke, Liverpool, England. It opened in 1985. The park is situated alongside the A561 road near to Liverpool John Lennon Airport 5 mi south-east of Liverpool city centre.
Rating: 4.2/5


Liverpool ONE

Liverpool ONE is a shopping in the heart of Liverpool city centre. Frequently has functions and programs happening all year and has over 170 shops in it. Totally an explorable place for youths!
Rating:4.5/5


Metquarter

Metquarter doesn't have as many shops as most other malls but the shops they do have are well-adored, such as Mac and Llamasquare cosmetic stores, a lot of branded boutiques as well as cafes
Rating:4.0/5


Clayton Square

Clayton Square Shopping Centre is a cosmopolitian-style shopping mall with really nice architecture and fairly near to other attractions in Liverpool within walking distance.
Rating:4.0/5


Cavern Walk

Cavern Walk has small selection of fashion, beauty and jewellery stores in a boutique-style shopping mall. You can see the Beatles statue here
Rating:4.4/5


Liverpool Shopping Park

Liverpool Shopping Park is a retail mall which has an endless list of shops and is also good for grabbing a quick meal. Parking spaces are sufficient.
Rating:4.3/5
