From being one the most stunning city to housing the tallest fine dining restaurants in the UK, Liverpool is a place you must stop by for a visit. There's just so much going on. Not to mention, Liverpool's night life is some of the liveliest and was once voted the fourth hippest place in UK. Liverpool does have some very stunning buildings that's worth your visit. And if you're a fan of football, Liverpool and Everton clubs are from here too.

This doesnt beat the main reason you should visit Liverpool -Scousers! (That means Liverpool people~) Scousers are overwhelmingly friendly and inviting and are known to be very generous with giving tourists assistance. You won't be lost in Liverpool!
